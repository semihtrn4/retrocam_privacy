Privacy Policy — Style Retro Cam

Last updated: April 1, 2026

This privacy notice for Retrocam: Style Retro Cam ("we," "us," or "our") explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Retrocam is a vintage film camera app designed to capture stunning photos and videos with authentic retro tones, analog film grain, light leaks, and vintage filters — including 8mm video mode, dazz cam style effects, and iconic film models like Classic M, D Classic, CCD R, and D Exp.

Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the app.

Summary of Key Points

  • We collect device identifiers and purchase data primarily through RevenueCat to manage your subscription.
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties or use it for advertising.
  • Photos and videos you capture are saved locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.
  • All filter presets, film models, and edits you apply are processed entirely on-device.
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.
  • We do not knowingly collect data from users under 18 years of age.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

Data Collected Automatically

  • Device ID — Used by RevenueCat to identify your device for subscription management.
  • User ID — A RevenueCat-assigned identifier to link your account with your subscription status.
  • Purchase & Subscription History — Records of your Retrocam Pro subscription processed via Apple App Store and RevenueCat.
  • Product Interaction Data — Anonymized data about how you use the app (e.g., filters used, camera modes activated) to improve the platform.
  • Crash & Performance Data — Technical diagnostics including crash logs, launch times, and performance metrics to ensure app stability.

Data You Provide

  • Retrocam does not require account registration or login.
  • If you contact our support team, we may collect your email address and the content of your message solely to respond to your inquiry.

Photos & Videos

📸 All photos and videos captured with Retrocam — including those with vintage filters, film grain, light leaks, and retro effects applied — are saved directly to your device (Camera Roll / Photos library). We do not upload, access, or store your photos or videos on our servers.

🎞 All film filters, color tone edits, vignette, and frame processing are performed entirely on your device. No image data is transmitted to external servers during editing or capture.

Camera Access

Retrocam requests access to your device's camera to capture photos and videos with vintage film effects. This permission is used exclusively for the core functionality of the app. You may revoke this permission at any time in your device Settings.

Payment Information

All payments are processed directly through the Apple App Store. We do not have access to your payment card details, bank information, or billing address. RevenueCat receives only confirmation of your subscription status.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. Subscription records managed by RevenueCat are retained in accordance with their data retention policy. Anonymized usage and diagnostic data may be retained for up to 12 months.

8. How Do We Keep Your Information Safe?

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), access controls, and regular security reviews. However, no system is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

9. Do We Collect Data from Minors?

Retrocam is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

13. Do-Not-Track
